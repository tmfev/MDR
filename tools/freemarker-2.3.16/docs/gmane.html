Gmane

Thanks to Gmane, our mailing lists are accessible with news interface as well. Gmane not only provides a two way mail to news interface, but let you read (browse and search) the mail archives with Web interface.

Browse/search the mailing list archives

Choose mailing list:

  • freemarker-user@lists.sourceforge.net
  • freemarker-devel@lists.sourceforge.net

News interfaces

All interfaces are two-way (you can both write and read).

  • For freemarker-user@lists.sourceforge.net:
    nntp://news.gmane.org/gmane.comp.web.freemarker.user
  • For freemarker-devel@lists.sourceforge.net:
    nntp://news.gmane.org/gmane.comp.web.freemarker.devel
